GroupGen

- A website that allows users to generate groupchats with AI models that are fully customizable and generate images depicting the conversations.

- Features intuitive and engaging UI/UX, with a focus on user experience and accessibility through unique and engaging features, as well as an easily scalable and secure backend infrastructure.

- Built using React.js, Node.js, Google Firebase, Stable Diffusion API, and Mistral API.

- Developed over the course of 3 months as a solo project, and was fully deployed to approximately 100 users.

- Hosted on Google Cloud with enterprise scaling and security, and can be accessed at https://groupgen-39162.uc.r.appspot.com/
